First 3 Steps to Sobriety

  1. Acknowledge You Have a Problem
  2. Seek Support
  3. Build New Habits

These are the foundation on which you will build your new life. Master these, and everything starts to change.

Acknowledge You Have a Problem

This is the most important step. Be honest with yourself and admit there is a problem. Write down your thoughts and feelings. Talk to someone you trust. The truth is where change begins.

Seek Support

You don't have to do this alone. Support can come from therapy, meetings, friends, or family. Find what works for you and use it.

Build New Habits & a New Life

Sobriety is about creating a life you don't want to escape from. Start small — one week sober, a new routine, a new hobby. Every small win builds confidence.
